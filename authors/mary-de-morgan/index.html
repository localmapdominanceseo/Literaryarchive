Mary De Morgan

1850–1907 British Victorian Fiction

Mary De Morgan was born in 1850 in London, sister of the artist William De Morgan and friend of William Morris. She wrote three collections of fairy tales celebrated for their moral complexity and literary quality. She died in Cairo in 1907.
