Matsuo Bashō

1644–1694 Japanese Japanese Haiku

Matsuo Bashō (1644-1694) is the master of the Japanese haiku form, which he elevated from a comic social game into a serious literary art. His most famous haiku — 'Old pond / a frog jumps in / sound of water' — is considered the founding text of haiku as understood today. He spent much of his later life on journeys, of which The Narrow Road to the Deep North is the masterpiece.
