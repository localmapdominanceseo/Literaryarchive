Ursula K. Le Guin

1929–2018 American American Modernism

Ursula K. Le Guin was born on October 21, 1929, in Berkeley, California. One of the greatest writers of speculative fiction, her Earthsea novels, The Left Hand of Darkness (1969), The Dispossessed (1974), and her short stories transformed science fiction and fantasy. She died in Portland, Oregon, in 2018.
