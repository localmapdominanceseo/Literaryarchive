Maya Angelou

1928–2014 American American Modernism

Maya Angelou was born on April 4, 1928, in St. Louis, Missouri, and became one of the most celebrated American poets and memoirists of the twentieth century, best known for her autobiography I Know Why the Caged Bird Sings (1969) and her poem Still I Rise. She recited her poem On the Pulse of Morning at President Clinton's inauguration in 1993. She died in Winston-Salem, North Carolina, in 2014.
