Dante Gabriel Rossetti

1828–1882 British Victorian Poetry

Dante Gabriel Rossetti was born on May 12, 1828, in London, and became both a celebrated painter and poet, a central figure in the Pre-Raphaelite Brotherhood. His poems, including the sonnet sequence The House of Life, are among the most elaborately crafted of the Victorian period. He died at Birchington-on-Sea in 1882.
