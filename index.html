Frequently Asked Questions

Are the poems on Literary Archive free to read?

Yes. Every poem published on Literary Archive is in the public domain and free to read in full, with no paywall or registration required.

Is Literary Archive affiliated with any publisher or school?

No. Literary Archive is an independent reference site. It is not affiliated with any publisher, university, or copyright holder.

How do you choose which poems to include?

We feature poems that are confirmed to be in the public domain in the United States, cross-checked against sources such as the Academy of American Poets and the Poetry Foundation before publication.
