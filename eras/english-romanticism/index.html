English Romanticism

English Romanticism emerged in the late eighteenth century as a reaction against industrialization and Enlightenment rationalism, championing nature, emotion, and the imagination. William Wordsworth, along with Samuel Taylor Coleridge, helped found the movement with the 1798 publication of Lyrical Ballads.

Poets in this era: John Keats · Lord Byron · Percy Bysshe Shelley · Robert Burns · Samuel Taylor Coleridge · Thomas Gray · William Blake · William Wordsworth
